## **Three Target Tasks for FL-02 through FL-04**



&#x20;Task 1: Drafting project proposals

"Done well" definition: An initial draft is generated that is more than 70% of the way to a final version, requiring only minor tone and specificity adjustments. The proposal structure is logical and aligned with the brief's requirements.



Task 2: Creating project status reports

"Done well" definition: A comprehensive first draft is produced that synthesizes data into clear, concise bullet points. The draft accurately highlights key risks and milestones that I must know about, saving me 1-2 hours of manual data compilation.



&#x20;Task 3: Brainstorming marketing ideas

"Done well" definition: A collaborative brainstorming session where the AI generates at least 10 relevant, non-obvious ideas that serve as a springboard for my own thinking, effectively ending my procrastination phase.
